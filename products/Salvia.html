Salvia

The 'Salvia' dress is a stunning gown that combines elegance and versatility. It features a beautifully beaded bodice that adds a touch of sparkle, complemented by removable long sheer sleeves. The light tulle skirt has a tasteful cutout, enhancing its graceful silhouette. The dress has a deep V-neckline and an open back for an elegant style.
